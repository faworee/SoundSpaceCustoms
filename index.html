Map Submissions & Approval
  • When you submit a map, it must be approved before it becomes publicly available. Maps are reviewed by me (faworee) or by other trusted people who have access to the map submissions panel.
  • Q: How long does approval take?
    A: Approval time can vary. Most maps are reviewed within 24 hours, but if I'm busy, it may take up to one week.
    • Data & Privacy
  • I do not collect, store, sell, or share any personal data. This website is a passion project, fully designed, developed, and managed by me alone.
    • Map Copy Tracking
  • If you copy a map while map copy tracking is enabled, the popularity count of that map will increase by 1. Abusing this feature may result in an IP ban from the website.
    • Login with Discord
  • When you log in using Discord, the application only accesses basic public information such as:
    • User ID
    • Username
    • Avatar
  • Nothing else is accessed — no email, no server joins, and no additional permissions.
  • Discord logins are saved so you don't have to log in repeatedly.
    • Cookies
  • Cookies are only used for:
    • Favoriting maps
    • Loading fonts
  • If cookies are declined, these features will not work properly.
